A clan Lv. 7 or above may establish an Order of Knights with 10,000 Clan Reputation points.
You can create up to 4 Orders of Knights. Each can have up to 10 clan members.
A clan that reaches Lv. 9 may increase the number of members in its Orders of Knights with 5,000 Clan Reputation points. Up to Lv. 10, they can do so for 2 Orders of Knights.
Upon creation of an Order of Knights, 1 Captain must be appointed from among its members. The social class of the newly-appointed Captain will go up.

Possession of a Departed Soul and Its Danger:
A Warcryer communes with dead souls. The ritual of a Warcryer involves recently departed souls from such tribes as Duda-mara, Hestui, and Gandi, and the souls of ancestors. The latter includes Orcs from the beginning of time, well-known souls such as Flame Lord Paagrio, and the power of indigenous souls belonging to each land. Each are selected and used according to the current need.
Occasionally, the ruling class of low-level Orcs commit forbidden acts for the sake of obtaining great power.
This forbidden magic technique is called Possession of the Departed Soul.

Gantaki Zu Urutu:
As you know, Khavatari is a title given to the greatest Kabart martial art masters. Khavatari Rosheek has left on a journey of trial to develop his skills to a higher level. He has not been seen in the village since. According to the rumors, he is devoting himself to discipline alone near the frozen waterfall in the northern part of the Immortal Plateau.
If you wish to learn from him, it will be good to prepare a gift. Take this pomegranate. It will not be easy, but if you perform all the tasks that he gives you, you will receive a scroll! Bring that back to me!
I must tell you one thing more before you leave! While you are taking the test to become a Paagrio Monk, you must not use any weapons such as a knife or an axe! You are only allowed to use your bare fists or hand-to-hand weapons! If you break this rule, you cannot complete the test! Remember what I have told you!

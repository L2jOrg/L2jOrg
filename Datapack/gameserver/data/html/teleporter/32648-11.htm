News Informer:
Reed Field of Innadril should not be visited if you are not above Level 83!
It's very complicated in various aspects. For example, a bunch of Mucrokians, a part of Crokian Faction, have appeared from nowhere and occupied the Reed Field; a strange Dwarf called Dr. Helvetica is threatening to wipe out the Reed Field; and torches are lighted and the power of spirits are gathering in the southern Reed Field in order to reclaim the old whispers and quietness of the Reed Field.
Also, Mucrokians are strong! They seem to be a bit weaker against magic...
and there are people who had such a strange experience as if being possessed by a ghost by drinking suspicious medicine rarely dropped by them...!
Anyway, head to Field of Silence to meet that charismatic Dr. Helvetica, and to Field of Whisper to meet an Elf performing Natural Regression Movement!

Prefect Brukurse:
The Cave of Trials is also called the Founder's Cave. It is a sacred place created by the founder of our race, and it houses the remains of our ancestors.
In the beginning, great Pa'agrio used the seeds of fire to create our race. But the other gods feared our power, and ther joined forces to kill our creator. They drowned the Eternal Flame with water, the wind scattered the ashes of his body, and the earth covered every last trace of him.
But with his dying breath, Pa'agrio instructed us to become stronger. For thousands of years, we have struggled to obey his command. This place, the Cave of Trials, is where the living history of our race begins....

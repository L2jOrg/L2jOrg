Tataru Zu Hestui:
Tejakar Oroka! Greeting, young traveler. I am Tataru of the Hestui Tribe, one of the Shamans tasked with passing on the teaching of flame from the god Pa'agrio.
Orcish magic uses the authority of the sacred flame to force spirits to do our bidding -- unlike those weak-willed Elves, who workship spirits as though they were gods, or those dim-witted Humans, who regard magic as science!

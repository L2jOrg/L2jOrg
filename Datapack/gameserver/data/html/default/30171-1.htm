Magister Gaius:
My master's name was Metheus. He was an indomitable Human, unafraid of making mistakes in his quest for knowledge. His thousands of magical experiments have borne bountiful fruit, transforming how many of us now see magic. Perhaps his most useful research concerned the "magic boost". It has often been misused, but working with his original discoveries we have begun to develop some more stable refinements. Perhaps you've heard of the spiritshot?
